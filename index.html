About Me

I am Rex Devasahayam Arokia Balaya, a Ph.D. holder from Yenepoya (Deemed to be University), Mangalore, India. As an Assistant Professor, my focus has been on studying interleukins and their role in cellular signaling related to inflammatory diseases. Specifically, I employ a quantitative phosphoproteomic approach to investigate the temporal dynamics of IL-33-mediated signaling in monocytes.

Assistant Professor.

My expertise encompasses various aspects of proteomics, including sample preparation, instrumentation, data interpretation, and unraveling signaling pathways
